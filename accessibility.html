Accessibility Statement

Site Clarity is committed to making this website accessible and easy to use for as many people as possible.

The website has been designed to follow recognised accessibility and usability principles, including clear structure, good colour contrast, and support for keyboard navigation.

We aim to align with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA where possible.
